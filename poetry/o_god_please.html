O God Please

O God, please

Bring peace to the Middle East

Bring peace to these streets,

To these minds, give us time

To remember what we came to do,

To remember that without love, we lose

Give us the required grinding courage as the

many mindless flourish and the

Jealous skin is ripped away

that kept us from Grace,

keep us safe

on our bikes and in our cars

and in our minds and in our hearts

and keep us far from worry

about money, about time, about worry

make us unafraid of our own fear

so that we will say and it will hear:

no it's my turn now

you don't tell me how

anymore.

Thank you, Lord.
